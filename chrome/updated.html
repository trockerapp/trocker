Trocker has been updated! Changes are listed below.

If you like Trocker and would like to support it, you can:

  • Report any issues with Trocker by emailing us at trockerapp@gmail.com or opening an issue on GitHub.
    • If you think an email is tracked but Trocker doesn't block it, remove your personal info and forward it to us at trockerapp@gmail.com.
    • Thanks for reporting issues. Given that Trocker needs to react to any breaking changes in webmails, we may not know about new issues unless you report them. Also, please consider giving us a chance to fix the issues before leaving a bad rating/review.
  • Consider rating Trocker on the Chrome Webstore and/or the Firefox add-ons page.
  • Consider supporting Trocker on BuyMeACoffee or Patreon.
  • Tell your friends about Trocker, for example by Tweeting (X'ing?) about it!
  • Follow @trockerapp
